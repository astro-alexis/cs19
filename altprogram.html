Preliminary Program

The workshop will start at 08:45 on Monday the 06th and end at lunch time on Friday the 10th. There will be a public lecture on Wednesday evening, and a banquet is organized for Thursday evening.
The workshop schedule is presented below.


  • Monday
  • Tuesday
  • Wednesday
  • Thursday
  • Friday

(sub)Stellar Interiors & Fundamental Properties

Morning: opening plenary session
Invited speakers:

Gilles Chabrier Progress & Limitations in (sub)Stellar Interior Models
Jacqueline Faherty TBD

Afternoon: splinter sessions

  • Mass-losing AGB Stars and Supergiants (part 1)
  • Star Clusters from Space, from the Ground, and Over Time (part 1)
  • Surface Magnetism of Cool Stars
  • The Brown Dwarf to Exoplanet Connection

Morning: plenary session
Invited speakers:

Caroline Morley Current Status of Modeling Atmospheres of Substellar Objects

Afternoon: splinter sessions
Mass-losing AGB Stars and Supergiants (part 2)
Star Clusters from Space, from the Ground, and Over Time (part 2)
Flares in Time-Domain Surveys
Variability of Solar/Stellar Magnetic Activity (part 1)

Morning: plenary session
Invited speakers:

Afternoon: excursions
