Getting there

The CS19 opening reception will be held on Sunday 05 June 2015 from XX PM at the orangerie of the botanical gardens (Uppsala botaniska trädgården). The venue is within walking distance of the city center and most hotels. The bus stop "Slottsbacken" (bus lines 4, 6, 7, 11, 20) is located 200 meters from the gardens entrance. Timetables and a trip-planner can be found on the UL website.
